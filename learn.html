LAKKAVARAM

  • Lakkavaram is a village located in Thallur mandal of Prakasam district in Andhra Pradesh, India. It is situated 14km away from sub-district headquarter Tallur (tehsildar office) and 49km away from district headquarter Ongole. As per 2009 stats, Lakkavaram village is also a gram panchayat.
